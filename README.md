## 💡 The Problem
India has over 14 crore+ Demat accounts, yet the vast majority of retail investors are "flying blind." They rely on gut feelings, random tips, and news headlines because parsing through a dense, 50-page corporate filing or quarterly result is complex, intimidating, and time-consuming.

## 🛠️ The Solution: InvestRadar AI
InvestRadar AI is not just a summarizer; it is an **Autonomous Signal-Finder**. 
Powered by the blazing-fast Groq API and Llama 3.1, this agentic workflow directly ingests raw corporate PDFs, processes the unstructured financial data, and instantly outputs actionable investment intelligence.

### ✨ Key Features:
1. **Signal Extraction:** Categorizes findings into clear `Opportunity`, `Risk`, or `Neutral` signals.
2. **Actionable Decisions:** Provides direct `Buy`, `Sell`, or `Watch` recommendations based solely on the data.
3. **Source-Cited Reasoning:** Eliminates hallucination by pointing directly to the metrics and facts within the uploaded text.
4. **Audit Trail:** Provides a transparent, step-by-step logical breakdown of *why* the AI made that specific decision, building trust for the retail investor.
